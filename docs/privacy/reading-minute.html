Reading Minute — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Reading Minute ("we", "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and why. By using Reading Minute you agree to this policy.

Who we are

Developer: Gnomonn. Contact: gnomonn.lab@gmail.com.

Data we collect

  • The app itself collects no data. All quotes and book excerpts are bundled with the app and shown locally; there is no account, no sign-in, and no backend server.
  • The reading screen shows a banner ad served by Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, AdMob may collect the advertising ID, approximate location (derived from IP address), and app-interaction / diagnostic data. This collection is performed by Google, not by us. See how Google uses data from apps that use its services.

Data we do NOT collect

  • No name, email address, phone number, or contacts.
  • No precise location.
  • No photos, files, or other device content.
  • No reading history sent anywhere — which quotes you view stays on your device.

Advertising

Reading Minute shows a single banner ad, only on the reading screen, via Google AdMob. In regions where the GDPR or similar laws apply, the app asks for your consent through Google's User Messaging Platform before any ad is requested. You can use the app fully without ads loading (for example, offline or if consent is declined) — the ad space simply stays hidden.

Third-party services

  • Google AdMob — banner advertising. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Project Gutenberg — the "read the full book" link opens gutenberg.org in your browser. Their site has its own policies; we send them no data about you.

Data retention & deletion

We store no user data on any server, so there is nothing for us to retain or delete. Ad-related data handled by Google is subject to Google's retention policies; you can reset your device's advertising ID or opt out of ad personalization in your device settings. For any question or request, email gnomonn.lab@gmail.com.

Children

Reading Minute is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions or data requests: gnomonn.lab@gmail.com.
