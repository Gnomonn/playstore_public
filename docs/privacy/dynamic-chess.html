Dynamic Chess — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-23

Dynamic Chess ("we", "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and why. By using Dynamic Chess you agree to this policy.

Who we are

Developer: Gnomonn. Contact: gnomonn.lab@gmail.com.

Data we collect

  • Advertising data — our ad provider (Google AdMob) may collect a device advertising ID (AAID), approximate location (derived from IP address), and app interaction / diagnostic data in order to serve and measure rewarded ads.

Data we do NOT collect

  • Your name, email address, or phone number — the app has no accounts or sign-in.
  • Your contacts, photos, files, or precise GPS location.
  • Your gameplay data. Scores, rank progress, and settings are stored only on your device and never leave it.
  • Any information that identifies you personally.

Advertising

Dynamic Chess shows optional rewarded ads via Google AdMob (for example, to continue a run after losing). AdMob may use advertising and device identifiers to serve personalized or non-personalized ads and to measure performance. Where required (e.g. in the EEA/UK), we ask for your consent before serving personalized ads, and you can change this choice at any time from the in-app privacy settings. See Google's policy: policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Third-party services

  • Google AdMob — advertising. Privacy policy.
  • Unity — the game engine the app is built with. We do not enable Unity analytics or Unity online services in this app.

Data retention & deletion

We operate no servers and store no user data ourselves. Advertising data is handled by Google under its own retention policies. Local game data can be removed at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app. For any data request, contact gnomonn.lab@gmail.com.

Children

Dynamic Chess is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions or data requests: gnomonn.lab@gmail.com.
